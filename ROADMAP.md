# Work Points Tracker - Product Roadmap

## Overview

This roadmap outlines features to evolve Work Points Tracker from MVP to a polished productivity app for its target niche: freelancers, side hustlers, and self-learners who need motivation to put in extra work hours.

---

## Phase 1: Polish & Retention
*Make people stick around*

| Feature | Description | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Weekly/Monthly Stats | Dashboard showing hours worked, points earned, streaks maintained | High |
| Home Screen Widget | Quick-start timer, current streak, points balance at a glance | High |
| Streak Recovery | One-time "streak freeze" purchasable with points | Medium |
| Session Notes | Optional text field to log what you worked on | Medium |
| Dark Mode | System-aware dark theme | Medium |

### Success Metrics
- 7-day retention rate
- Average sessions per week
- Widget installation rate

---

## Phase 2: Motivation & Accountability
*Solve the "I'm my own boss" problem*

| Feature | Description | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Daily/Weekly Goals | Set targets like "10 hrs side work this week" with progress visualization | High |
| Push Notifications | Streak at risk alerts, wish item proximity alerts, daily reminders | High |
| Milestones & Celebrations | Confetti animations, achievement unlocks, milestone cards | Medium |
| Photo Proof for Wish Items | Capture photo when you actually purchase/receive wish item | Low |
| Export/Share Stats | Generate shareable cards for social media | Low |

### Success Metrics
- Goal completion rate
- Notification engagement rate
- Wish item redemption rate

---

## Phase 3: Insights & Intelligence
*Help users understand their patterns*

| Feature | Description | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Best Productive Hours | Analytics showing when user is most productive | Medium |
| Streak Calendar View | GitHub-style contribution/heat map | Medium |
| Points Projection | "At this pace, you'll afford X in Y weeks" | Medium |
| Session Categories/Tags | Categorize work: learning, freelance, creative, etc. | Low |

### Success Metrics
- Time spent in stats/insights screens
- User-reported "aha moments"

---

## Phase 4: Optional Social Layer
*For those who want accountability partners*

| Feature | Description | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Accountability Partner | Invite one person to see your streak status | Medium |
| Shared Wish List | Partner can see what you're saving for | Low |
| Weekly Check-in | Automated weekly summary to share with partner | Low |

### Design Principles
- Keep it intimate (1-2 partners, not a social network)
- Opt-in only
- No leaderboards or competition

### Success Metrics
- Partner connection rate
- Retention difference: partnered vs solo users

---

## Phase 5: Power User & Integrations
*For committed users*

| Feature | Description | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Toggl/Clockify Import | Import time entries from existing tools | Low |
| Calendar Integration | Auto-detect work sessions from calendar blocks | Low |
| Custom Point Rules | Let users adjust point rates for their situation | Low |
| CSV Export | Export session history for records/invoicing | Medium |
| Apple Watch / Wear OS | Start/stop timer from wrist | Low |

### Success Metrics
- Integration adoption rate
- Power user retention (90+ day)

---

## Anti-Features (What We Won't Build)

| Feature | Why Not |
|---------|---------|
| Full Social Network | Distracts from personal productivity focus |
| Public Leaderboards | Creates unhealthy competition, discourages beginners |
| Excessive Badge Types | Dilutes meaning and achievement satisfaction |
| AI Features (for the sake of AI) | Must solve real problems, not add complexity |
| Ads | Undermines trust and user experience |

---

## Recommended Priority Order

### Immediate (Next Release)
1. Home Screen Widget
2. Session Notes
3. Weekly Stats View

### Short Term (1-2 months)
4. Push Notifications (streak at risk)
5. Daily/Weekly Goals
6. Dark Mode

### Medium Term (3-4 months)
7. Streak Calendar View
8. Points Projection
9. Milestones & Celebrations
10. Streak Recovery (freeze)

### Long Term (6+ months)
11. Accountability Partner
12. Integrations (Toggl, Calendar)
13. Wearable Support

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:**
- Indie hackers and side project builders
- Freelancers managing time outside client work
- Developers learning new skills after hours

**Secondary:**
- Students with self-directed study goals
- Creatives working on personal projects
- Anyone with a "someday" project they keep postponing

---

## Core Value Proposition

> "Turn your extra effort into tangible rewards. Every hour of side work gets you closer to something you actually want."

The app succeeds when users:
1. Feel motivated to start sessions
2. Feel rewarded when they complete them
3. Experience genuine satisfaction redeeming wish items
4. Build sustainable work habits (not burnout)
